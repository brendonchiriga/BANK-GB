We help you solve your problem with peace and serenity,quickly. That means we contact you emmediately after you report to us. However we are expert in solving all the Gender Based Violation issues. This is where you meet the best lawyers and social workers you can trust.




The Introduction

A victim’s greatest fear is not being believed, too many people who have been victimised are too afraid to come forward because they believe nobody will listen to them or they want to be anonymous because they fear what might happen or be said after. We create a safe space for people have been victimised, our goal is to encourage them to speak up and we promise that they will be heard so we can get perpetrators accountable, so they will not be able to hurt other people and justice is served. We promise to stand with everyone who report the crimes and offer services to help with counselling, where victims will be helped emotionally with the trauma. We also offer a 24/7 days-a-week help centre for victims who would like assistance. We can never do enough to eradicate this huge problem our society faces; its time action is taken (ENOUGH IS ENOUGH).
